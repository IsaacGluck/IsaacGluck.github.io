Personal Website (aka this one)
I built this website using HTML5 Boilerplate, Bootstrap and jQuery. I also used Stellar.js for the parallax feature, and Verify.js and Notify.js for the form. For the portfolio picture hover effects I used some neat ideas from Codedrops.

Thanks so much for checking it out!
Darby Doomsday Website
The Darby Doomsday website is a partner website to the Darby Doomsday book series written by Professor Ernest Hebert. The website contains sneak peeks to the books, a gallery of artwork pertaining to the series, an interactive map, and sections where the author can share blog posts, essays, and poems.

I helped to develop the website with a team from the Digital Arts, Leadership, & Innovation Lab. I worked on the gallery section, the interactive map, and mobile compatibility.

Hope you enjoy it!
